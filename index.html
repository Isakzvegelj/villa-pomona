The Villa

A Living Heritage

Villa Pomona is a meticulously restored heritage villa on the most picturesque street in Bled — just a 3-minute walk from the lake and town center.

Originally built as a private residence for a prominent Bled family, the villa has been thoughtfully transformed into a boutique retreat that honors its architectural legacy while offering modern luxury. Every room tells a story — from the original parquet floors to the hand-painted ceiling details.

Today, Villa Pomona offers five distinctive suites, each with its own character, a swimming pool with pool house, a traditional sauna, and a sprawling botanical garden that blooms from spring through autumn.

Unforgettable Experiences

From the villa, Bled's best attractions are just steps away. We can help you plan every moment of your stay.

⛶

Bled Island

Traditional pletna boat ride to the island with its iconic church. 3 min walk to the boat station.

🏰

Bled Castle

Medieval castle on a cliff with panoramic views. 10 min walk or 5 min by car.

🏔

Vintgar Gorge

Stunning 1.6 km wooden walkway through a dramatic canyon. 2.4 km from Bled.

🚴

Lake Cycling

6 km paved path around Lake Bled. Bicycle rental available at the villa.

⛰

Julian Alps

Hiking, climbing, and paragliding in Triglav National Park. 30 min drive.

🍰

Kremšnita Tasting

Try Bled's iconic cream cake at the original source. A must for food lovers.

Our Commitment

⚡

Solar Energy

Solar water heating system reduces our carbon footprint while keeping the pool and suites warm.

🌱

Local Sourcing

Organic, locally sourced breakfast products and seasonal ingredients from nearby farms and producers.

🚴

E-Bike Rental

LED Lighting

Energy-efficient LED lighting throughout the villa and grounds reduces electricity consumption.
