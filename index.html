About Villa Pomona

Villa Pomona is a historic villa with distinctive Art Nouveau architecture, restored vintage elements, and untouched natural surroundings.

Located in a quiet part of Bled, just a short walk from the lake. It offers the perfect retreat for couples, families, and anyone seeking relaxation in nature.
