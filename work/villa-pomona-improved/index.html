About Villa Pomona

Villa Pomona is a historic villa with distinctive Art Nouveau architecture, restored vintage elements, and untouched natural surroundings.

Located in a quiet part of Bled, just a short walk from the lake. It offers the perfect retreat for couples, families, and anyone seeking relaxation in nature.

Nearby Attractions

Bled and its surroundings offer endless opportunities for exploration, relaxation, and adventure.

Lake Bled

The iconic lake with its island church. 3 min walk from the villa.

3 min walk

Vintgar Gorge

A natural wonder with crystal-clear river and walking path. 30 min drive.

30 min drive

Triglav National Park

Slovenia's largest national park with stunning alpine nature. 45 min drive.

45 min drive

Lake Bohinj

A quieter, less crowded lake perfect for kayaking and swimming. 40 min drive.

40 min drive

Julian Alps

Stunning mountain area for hiking, cycling, and skiing.

15-60 min drive

Bled Castle

The oldest castle in Slovenia on a magnificent cliff above the lake. 30 min walk.
