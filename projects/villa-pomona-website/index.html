Our Story

A Century of Alpine Elegance

Nestled in the heart of Bled, Villa Pomona has been a sanctuary of refined hospitality since 1920. Our heritage villa, surrounded by century-old gardens, offers a rare blend of historic charm and contemporary comfort.

Each of our seven suites is individually designed, honoring the villa's Art Nouveau heritage while providing modern luxury. From the manicured gardens to the lake-facing terraces, every detail tells a story of Slovenian grace.

Moments at Villa Pomona

Experiences

Discover Bled

From the villa's gardens to the Julian Alps, Bled offers unforgettable experiences year-round.

🏰

Bled Castle

Perched on a cliff above the lake, Slovenia's oldest castle offers panoramic views and a fascinating museum.

🚣

Pletna Boat

Traditional wooden boats to Bled Island, where you can ring the wishing bell in the 17th-century church.

🥾

Vintgar Gorge

A stunning 1.6km gorge with wooden walkways along turquoise waters, just 4km from the villa.

⛷️

Julian Alps

World-class hiking in summer, skiing in winter. Triglav National Park is a short drive away.

🍰

Kremšnita

Taste Bled's famous cream cake at the original bakery since 1953. A must-try Slovenian delicacy.

🍷

Wine Regions

Explore Slovenia's diverse wine regions — Goriška Brda, Štajerska, and Kras are all within easy reach.
